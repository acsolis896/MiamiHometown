Enjoy culture and food in this vibrant, tropical city in South Florida!

Top Three Activities to Do in Miami

Go to one of the many beaches

Nothing's better than a beach day in Miami with come cold drinks and a sandwich from Publix.

Eat at the hottest restaurants

Miami is a hub for all kinds of cuisine. Lincoln Road & Wynwood are great neighborhoods to grab a bite.

Shopping is a must in the city

Luxury, vintage, or artsy buys...Miami has it all. Hit up the Design District or Coral Gables for the atmosphere and shops!
